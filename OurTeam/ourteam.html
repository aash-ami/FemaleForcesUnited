Larissa who was born and raised in a small town in Cameroon called Essimba, created Female Force United back in February 2019 with the intent of raising money towards donations to the Central African Country of Cameroon. With only a small group of Women, they were able to volunteer at local food banks and also held community auctions along with friendly sales to achieve this goal. As the Non Profit Organization grew even more, they began extending their donations into other coutnries of Aftrica of all different regions.
