Community Outreach

Community Outreach in Gambia

FFU came together to give what the children of New Life Orphanage deserve. This orphanage, located in Gambia, puts the value of human life as the central core value and concern. It was such a beautiful experience making a positive impact in the life of orphans. These children deserve opportunities in life to become the most successful adults of tomorrow.

Women Who Care Ministry Outreach

On MLK Jr. day, the FFU's Ladies joined the women's who care Ministries in Gaithersburg, packed food for the orphans in Montgomery County, Maryland.

Community Outreach in Congo

The ladies of FFU joined hands once again to bring our modest contributions to the La Compassion Orphanage in Congo to contribute to their mission to bring up healthier children. Every child deserves the best nutrition and education among other things to become a valuable citizen of tomorrow. A big thank you to Orphelinat la Compassion, the ladies who devoted so much of their time to help us realize this project in Congo during the Holiday season and of course we thank God for providing. To the dedicated team at Female Force United, kudos.

Community Outreach in Guinea

Community Outreach in Cameroon

The ladies at FFU got the opportunity to show their support to a local orphanage in Cameroon. We assisted a new mother as she struggled to provide food shelter and clothing to these children. Although she has been able to provide shelter to over 90 children since she opened her door years ago, we understand she could use a helping hand. We were represented a group of young ladies in Bafoussam and brought our contributions to our noble cause. Together, we were able to provide non-perishable food to help feed these children.
