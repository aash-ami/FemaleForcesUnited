This year, the organization will be hosting a gala event supporting maternal and child healthcare.

This gala will take place at the Salvation Army community center in Germantown MD.

Raffles will be held where guests would be able to win prizes, there will be auctions for certain highly priced items, and there will be entertainers such as dancers and motivational speakers.

The organization is currently in collaboration with a few sponsors such as Advanced Functional Health and Wellness which is a clinic located in Frederick MD, Purpose One Realty which is a real estate firm in the DMV area, and a bunch more!
