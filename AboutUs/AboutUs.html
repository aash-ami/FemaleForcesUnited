About FFU

Female Force United is a nonprofit organization containing a group of women from different African backgrounds. In this organization, the women come together sharing a vision of creating a positive impact in the community from the U.S. and abroad. For the past 4 years now, the non profit organization has been able to raise money in order to donate food items, cleaning supplies, bed mattresses,and school supplies to ophanages in Ghana, Gambia, Cameroon, Burkina Faso, Guinea, and Congo.

The ngo's mission is to support citizens in need at the community level globally.

The organization advocates for healthy living within the community so that people could live a healthy lifestyle. To carry out this goal, they work to empower individuals and communities to make healthier choices and to excercise more.

The organization cooperates with parents to ensure that every child has the best resources and opportunities for them to reach their full learning potential.

They help homeless women and children rebuild their lives and work towards reducing the amount of homelessness in the community.
