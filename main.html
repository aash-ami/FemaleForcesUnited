Female Force United is a nonprofit organization containing a group of women from different African backgrounds. In this organization, the women come together sharing a vision of creating a positive impact in the community from the U.S. and abroad.

For the past 4 years now, the non profit organization has been able to raise money in order to donate food items, cleaning supplies, bed mattresses,and school supplies to ophanages in Ghana, Gambia, Cameroon, Burkina Faso, Guinea, and Congo.

In September of 2019 the organization volunteered at a health fair aiding another local non profit organization provide health screenings to their community that lacked health insurance.

During that same month,the ngo volunteered at a local food pantry packaging imperishable food items.

On Januarary 18, of 2021, which was also MLk Jr.Day, the members collaborated with the Women Who Care Ministries in Gaithersburg packing food for the orphans.

The organization went on to and is still continuing to make donations to their local community and in impoverished areas of Africa.

Collaborating with Female Force United always for other non-profit organizations to benefit from this by having FFU helping too host fundraiser and donations with them along with extending their audience.
