Female Force United is a nonprofit organization comprising of a group of about 20 women coming from different African backgrounds. In this organization, the like minded women get together working towards making a positive impact in the community in the U.S. and abroad.

Fast forwarding 4 years now, the non profit organization has been able to raise money in which has been used to donate food items, cleaning supplies, bed mattresses,and school supplies to ophanages in Ghana, Gambia, Cameroon, Burkina Faso, Guinea, and Congo.

In September of 2019, the organization volunteered at a health fair and collaborated with another local non profit organization providing health screenings to uninsured communities all over Maryland.

Later that same month,the ngo volunteered at a local food pantry that was going on in their county packaging imperishable food items for families who were in need.

On Januarary 18, of 2021, MLk Jr.Day, the members joined alongside the Women Who Care Ministries in Gaithersburg and packed food cans for the orphans.

The organization went on to and is still continuing to make donations to their local community and in impoverished areas of Africa.

Collaborating with Female Force United always for other non-profit organizations to benefit from this by having FFU helping too host fundraiser and donations with them along with extending their audience.
