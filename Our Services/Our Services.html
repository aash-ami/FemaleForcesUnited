Our Services

We help the less fortunate starting at the community level to around the world, hold mutual reward for giver and receiver while sharing with the spirit of love, and advocate for healthy living within our community and beyond.

Health

We encourage healthy living by empowering individuals and communities to take action for their health.

Education

Every child deserves the opportunity to learn. Therefore, we work with parents every day to ensure all children have the best chance to learn.

Homelessness

We help homeless women and their children rebuild their lives and break the cycle of homelessness.

Female Rights

As women, we are all entitled to human rights. These include the right to live free from violence and discrimination, to enjoy the highest attainable standard of physical and mental health, to be educated; to own property, to vote, and to earn an equal wage.
