September 2019

Volunteered at a food pantry, packaging nonperishable foods for low income families.

July 2021

FFU reached out and donated non-perishable food to CAED Sainte Therese De L' Enfant Jesus De Loumbila Orphanage in Burkina-Faso.

December 2022

Donated stationery items, food, and cleaning supplies to help provide the basic needs of the children at Akwadum Christian Village Orphanage, Koforidua, Eastern Region, Ghana.

July 2022

FFU donated nutritional foods to the La Compassion Orphanage in Congo. The organization wanted to ensure that every child there was eating food that would preserve their health and keep them healthy.

December 2023

Female Force United volunteered at the annual Christmas Drive hosted at Church of the Redeemer. The organization helped carry out boxes containing canned foods into cars during drive thrus. In addition, they prayed for people who wanted blessing for the new upcoming year.

Januarary 2021

On Martin Luther King Jr. Day, Female Force United went and volunteered at the Women Who Care Ministry located in Gaithersburg, MD and helped packed food for orphans in Montgomery County, MD.

July 2023

FFU, went and collaborated with Generosity Global where they cleaned mobile showers each time a user finished taking their shower. In addition, they helped lead people who were in need into the shower trucks.

August 2022

Female Force United was able to support a new mother in Bafoussam, Cameroon who was able to provide a home to more than 90 children by sending her non-perishable foods that she would use to help feed the children.

October 2019

The non profit organization volunteered at a local Women's shelter in Gaithersburg, MD where they helped make goodie bags containing personal hygiene, deodorant, lotion, hand sanitizer, and other personal care supplies for women who were in lack of those items.
